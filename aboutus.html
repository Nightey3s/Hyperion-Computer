About Us

Hyperion Computer was founded in 2016 by a team of gamers and pc enthusiasts. Our mission is simple, to deliver a unique and custom computer that suit every customer's needs, combined with bleeding edge technology and professional craftsmanship. This is because, a computer is not just a computer, rather it's a work of art, and we are here to help you make that art a reality. We provide services such as custom built pc, upgrading of your exisitng system. You can also buy the prebuilt systems that we showcase.

Our Focus

Performance

Putting together a custom gaming pc is only part of the process, we are obsessed with squeezing every ounce of performance from our computers. Anyone can put a system together, however to unlock maximum performance, factors such as: the wiring, airflow, power delivery, and layout of components represents the core foundation of a true custom PC.

Craftsmanship

Every gaming PC is hand built by skilled technicians. Everything from inventory allocation, assembly, testing, and quality assurance are done in-house to ensure we provide only the very best for our customers. This is what makes a gaming computer truly special, being handmade with perfection and finished with utmost care.

Testing

High-performance hardware needs to be properly tested for bottlenecks and stability issues. Our team of technicians test every custom gaming computer with a series of stress-tests and benchmarks that analyze all the components. Once the gaming PC passes these tests, it goes through a second round of testing to ensure the performance is consistent before sending the computer to our quality assurance team.

Support

Every purchase comes with lifetime expert support by our in-house technicians. We strive to resolve any issue with your gaming computer as quickly and efficiently as possible. With round the clock support available, you can be assured that at any time, there will be someone to answer your queries. We imagine the situation from the customer’s shoes, and treat them just like we would want ourselves to be treated.
