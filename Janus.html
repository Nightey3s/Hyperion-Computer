Tiny just got tinier

By reducing every space possible, the Janus, has only a 7 litre capacity, yet it performs just as well as most gaming desktops.

Zero Compromise

The Janus can fit a desktop CPU and a full size graphics card in its enclosure. Worries of performance loss are a thing of the past.

Fully Vented

Careful design and precise engineering allows Janus to have no case fans at all, rather, relying on the openess of the case to let air in.

Packed with features

Wireless connection, bluetooth, high capacity storage, desktop performance and a power supply, all within a 7 litre capacity.
